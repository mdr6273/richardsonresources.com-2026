Who We Help

Built for Small Operations

Small businesses that need reliable IT support without the cost or complexity of an in-house team or a large managed services provider.

The Right Fit

Running Lean and Need IT That Matches

Richardson Resources is a single-person operation, so it's naturally suited for clients of a similar scale. The work has always been focused on small businesses—the kind where the owner is also the one answering the phone, handling the day-to-day, and figuring out the technology along the way.

If you need IT help that fits your size—not a scaled-down version of enterprise support—this tends to be a comfortable fit.

  • Solo operators and independent professionals
  • Teams of 2 to 10 people
  • Small local businesses and service providers
  • Startups getting their first real tech setup right
  • Anyone who outgrew personal email but isn't ready for enterprise software
