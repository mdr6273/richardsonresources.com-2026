What Makes These Sites Different

Every site in this portfolio was built with the same approach: clean code, no unnecessary dependencies, and a structure that's easy to update months or years later.

Most small business websites get built, then abandoned—hard to update, built on tools nobody remembers how to use. These are built differently.
