Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here is how we handle things.

Short Version (Human-Readable)

  • We collect only the information we need to work with you (contact info, project files, system data, etc.).
  • We never sell, share, or give your data to anyone unless required by law.
  • We keep:
    1. Project files and development work indefinitely
    2. Customer information (including billing and contact info) indefinitely
  • We will delete your data if:
    1. Our contract requires it, or
    2. You request it (after services are no longer active)
Important: Ownership of Work
  • Unless our contract says otherwise:
    1. We retain ownership of all code, systems, and development assets I create
    2. You retain ownership of your pre-existing materials (branding, content, business data, etc.)
  • Your project is never shared or reused in identifiable form.
  • We may reuse general techniques or non-identifiable components, or create templates, as long as they do not contain your confidential information.

If you want different ownership or retention terms, they must be clearly defined in the contract.

Full Privacy Policy (Legal Version)

Effective Date: April 20, 2026   Business Name: Richardson Resources   Location: California, United States

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Richardson Resources ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with IT consulting, software development, and managed services provided to clients throughout the United States.

This Policy also clarifies default data retention and intellectual property ownership practices, unless otherwise modified by written agreement.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and maintain the following categories of information:

a. Client Information

  • Name, business name, email, phone number, billing details, and related records

b. Project and Development Data

  • Source code, software, scripts, configurations, documentation, credentials, and other materials provided by the client or created during the course of services

c. Managed Services Data

  • System data, logs, backups, network information, and operational data necessary to provide ongoing services

d. Communications

  • Emails, messages, and other correspondence

3. Use of Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Deliver contracted services
  • Maintain continuity of projects and systems
  • Communicate with clients
  • Support and troubleshoot systems

We do not use client data for resale, advertising, or profiling.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We maintain a strict no-sharing policy:

  • We do not sell, rent, or share client data with third parties
  • We do not disclose client data except:
    • With explicit client authorization, or
    • When required by law

5. Data Retention

a. General Policy

We retain client data and project materials indefinitely unless otherwise specified in a written agreement or upon valid client request.

b. Development Projects

All development-related materials (including code and technical artifacts) are retained indefinitely.

c. Client and Billing Information

Client contact information, billing records, and related data are retained indefinitely, unless:

  • A contract specifies a different retention period, or
  • The client requests deletion after services have concluded and no active billing relationship remains

d. Deletion Requests

Clients may request deletion of their data. We will comply within a reasonable timeframe, subject to:

  • Legal or accounting obligations
  • Backup and archival system limitations

6. Intellectual Property and Ownership

a. Contractor Ownership (Default)

Unless otherwise explicitly stated in a written agreement:

  • All software, code, systems, scripts, configurations, and related development assets created by us remain our sole intellectual property
  • We retain full rights to reuse, modify, license, or otherwise use such materials

b. Client Ownership

Clients retain ownership of:

  • Pre-existing intellectual property
  • Business data
  • Branding, content, and materials provided by the client

c. Deliverables

No transfer of intellectual property rights is implied by delivery of work product unless explicitly stated in a written contract.

d. Reuse and Templates

We may:

  • Reuse generalized knowledge, techniques, and non-identifiable components
  • Create templates or derivative works only if they do not contain client-identifiable or confidential information

e. Confidentiality

Client-specific implementations, configurations, and data are treated as confidential and are not shared or disclosed.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect client data, including access controls and secure storage practices. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights (California and U.S.)

Under applicable laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information

We do not sell personal information.

Requests can be submitted using the contact information below.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised effective date.
